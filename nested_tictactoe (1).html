📋 Rules & Help

How to play:

  • Click any unlocked small board to play your X or O.
  • Win that small board to claim it.
  • Win a row, column, or diagonal of small boards to claim the middle board.
  • Win 3 middle boards in a row (in the big board) to win the match.

Drawn boards restart automatically. Undo & Hints are limited to 3 per game. The timer can be paused or reset.
